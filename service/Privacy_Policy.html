Privacy Policy for Monlam Elementary Dictionary

At Monlam IT, we understand the importance of privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information of our users. This privacy policy applies to the Monlam Elementary Dictionary mobile app, which is a free Tibetan language dictionary for Tibetan children.

Collection of Personal Information

Monlam Elementary Dict does not collect, store, or use any personal information from its users. The app does not require users to register or provide any personal information to use the app.

Use of Information

Monlam Elementary Dict does not use any information from its users for any purpose, including but not limited to, marketing, research, or analytics. The app is designed to provide a simple and user-friendly experience for Tibetan children to learn the Tibetan language.

Disclosure of Information

Monlam Elementary Dict does not share or disclose any information from its users to any third party, unless required to do so by law. In the event that we are required to disclose information, we will only do so in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Data Security

Monlam Elementary Dict is designed to be secure and protect the privacy of its users. The app does not store any information from its users, so there is no risk of unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of personal information.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Monlam IT may change this privacy policy at any time, and will provide notice of such changes by updating the privacy policy on the Monlam Elementary Dict app or by other means.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the use of personal information, please contact us at monlamit2012@mail.com .

This privacy policy is effective as of Saturday February 11, 2023.
